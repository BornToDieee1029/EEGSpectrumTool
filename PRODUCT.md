# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Clinicians and EEG researchers. They upload a patient's resting-state EEG and
read an objective measurement that supports — never replaces — their judgment.
Context: a clinical or research workstation, focused, evaluating one recording at
a time. Secondary audience: reviewers/judges and non-expert viewers who need to
understand what the tool does at a glance.

## Product Purpose

Place a resting-state EEG on a state-stability spectrum derived from microstate
dynamics — from the ordered calm of a Zen monk to the restless switching of ADHD.
It exists to give an objective, reproducible anchor for an otherwise
judgment-heavy assessment. Success = a clinician trusts the number enough to fold
it into their reasoning, and never mistakes it for a diagnosis.

## Brand Personality

Clinical and precise. Calm, trustworthy, restrained — it should read like a
well-made medical instrument or a lab report, not a product pitch. Quiet
confidence over flourish. Honesty is part of the voice: limitations are shown,
not hidden.

## Anti-references

- **Generic AI/SaaS demo** — no default-Streamlit look, rainbow accents, emoji,
  hero-metric clichés, or tiny tracked-uppercase eyebrow kickers.
- **Flashy consumer health app** — no bright gradients, oversized rounded cards,
  or gamified wellness styling.
- **Sterile hospital/EHR software** — no cold, cramped, dated enterprise look.

## Design Principles

- **Instrument, not interface.** Every element earns its place; the data leads,
  the chrome recedes.
- **Honest by construction.** Uncertainty, sample sizes, and caveats are visible
  in the UI, not buried.
- **Explain in place.** Each result and chart carries a plain-language reading so
  a non-specialist understands it without losing the professional terms.
- **Restraint over decoration.** Quiet typography and deliberate spacing do the
  work; color is used only to carry meaning.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA: body text ≥4.5:1 contrast, visible keyboard focus, readable sizes,
reduced-motion support. Color always paired with text/position so meaning never
depends on hue alone (relevant for the spectrum and group charts).
